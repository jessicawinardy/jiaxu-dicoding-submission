❓ Who is Xavier?

Xavier is a tall young man with grey-blonde hair, blue eyes, and fair skin. He is typically seen wearing a white sweater and dark pants. Despite his youthful physique, his real age is unknown.

Recently, many citizens have been talking about a mysterious hunter who rescued them from Wanderers. With his combat prowess, he took down very dangerous Wanderers and disappeared in the blink of an eye, as if pursuing something greater. We reached out to the witnesses. From their accounts, it seems their mysterious savior is Xavier, a Deepspace Hunter.

Life

He sometimes gets injured in missions, but barely ever visits the hospital to get his wounds treated. Alarm Clock, Fatso... He names every bird that frequently visits his house. He loves to fish and ended up befriending the uncle who runs the guesthouse because of this.

Past Life
